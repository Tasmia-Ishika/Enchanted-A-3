Event Planner.

An event or wedding planner is responsible for - you guessed it - planning the wedding or event. Planning implies a full logistical process of careful preparation over a period of time and encompasses much more than merely directing a ceremony or orchestrating details on the day of the wedding.

Wedding Planner

A professional who assists with the planning, design, & management of a wedding. While a wedding coordinator and wedding designer's responsibilities would come into play closer to your big day, a wedding planner would be with you every step of the way during the wedding planning process.
